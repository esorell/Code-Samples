ABOUT SUSAN

Susan is an Educational Consultant with a high level of expertise in advising students on post-secondary options in both Canada and the US. She graduated With Distinction from the College Admissions Certificate Program at UCLA Extension, having completed her practicum in the Guidance Department at the University of Toronto Schools. Her specialty is finding the best-fit school for each student by taking the time to delve into their strengths, interests, personality, motivation and dreams.


As a former Assistant Crown Attorney she has strong analytical and perceptual skills. She brings to the relationship a commitment to individualized one-on-one attention and a passion for researching and visiting colleges and universities. Susan is committed to keeping abreast of current trends and best practices in the industry through membership in organizations and groups and attendance at conferences. She has developed strong relationships with guidance and admission professionals in Canada and the US.


As a parent of three athletes and advisor to many others, she has a deep understanding of the unique needs of the student athlete and the importance of helping each one find the right academic, social and athletic environment. She takes the time to gain an awareness of the student from all of these angles to help them reach a thoughtful decision.


Susan’s ability to develop a trusting relationship with her students, though her approachable and encouraging manner, can help to ease the parent-child tension that is natural during this critical time of growing independence in a young adult’s life. She has the research skills to save parents and students’ time and energy, which allows them to focus on other aspects of the post-secondary application and decision-making process. Susan is passionate about working with her students to find the right fit and delights in the confidence and self-understanding that they gain along the way.


Susan lives in Oakville, Ontario with her husband and three children. She loves golf, skiing, fitness, reading and visiting campuses! She is also a Roseheart Reiki Master Healer and Meditation Facilitator. She has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Queen’s University, a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from the University of Western Ontario, a Certificate in French from the University of Grenoble and Post-Graduate Certificate in College Admissions Counseling from UCLA.
